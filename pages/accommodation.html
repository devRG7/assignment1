TRAVEL & ACCOMODATION

The following Hotels are available for accommodation near the venue:

Long Beach Mauritius

Long Beach Mauritius is a five-star resort, encompassed by dense tropical gardens on Belle Mare beach along the East Coast of Mauritius. The immaculate beach melting into the cyan water, makes Long Beach a top-tier holiday destination. Besides its prime location, the hotel can be described as an escape for travellers seeking a blissful haven where they can relax or enjoy certain activities and services in a contemporary luxury setting.

Le Marché Restaurant

At Long Beach's main restaurant, Le Marché, mornings start with a gourmet breakfast buffet featuring everything from fresh juices and yogurts to scrumptious pastries and other options. In the evenings, you get to enjoy international buffets with live cooking demonstrations that add a spectacular touch to the experience.
Opening Hours: Continental Breakfast 04:00 - 07:00 | Full English 07:00 - 10:00 | Dinner 18:30 - 22:00

Le Meridien lle Maurice

Located on the bay of Pointe aux Piments on the northwest coast of Mauritius, Le Méridien Ile Maurice offers a kilometre-long stretch of white sandy beaches and spectacular views of the warm turquoise waters of the Indian Ocean. The hotel features a plethora of tropical inspirations, from the local artwork in the lobby to the authentic flavours of the restaurants, gardens of Eden and sundowner balconies, creating the perfect atmosphere for an island vacation.

Nomad Restaurant

Nomad, known for its "à la minute" cuisine, is a restaurant that offers a menu of local and international dishes. The restaurant also has a terrace with a panoramic view of the Indian Ocean, ideal for an outdoor dinner. Guests can also witness the savoir-faire of the chefs through the open kitchen of the restaurant.
Opening hours: Breakfast 06:00 – 10:30 | Dinner 18:00 – 22:00

Radisson Blu Azuri Resort & Spa

Set on a preserved beach on the northeast of Mauritius, Radisson Blu Azuri Resort & Spa delivers a distinctive getaway experience to its guests. The rooms with furnished balconies and terraces overlook the verdant tropical surroundings Azuri village. For those who prefer to indulge in the culinary delicacies, the hotel offers a taste of Mauritius with its local menu while keeping pace with international favourites. Radisson Blu Azuri Resort & Spa can be anybody’s favourite.


Le Comptoir Restaurant

Buffet lovers will love Le Comptoir where food from around the world and local dishes are served and can be enjoyed in a stylish setup overlooking the pool.
Semi Buffet or Set Menu or Family Style menu (as indicated by the hotel).
Opening Hours: Breakfast 07:00 - 10:30 | Lunch 12:00 - 15:00 | Dinner 18:30 - 22:00
